Mental Math Challenge

Welcome to Mental Math Challenge, an interactive calculation game where you solve automatically generated math or date-based problems. Customize your quiz by providing a blueprint that defines the number, type, and structure of the questions.

You can create both math quizzes with expressions involving numbers, operators, functions, and scientific constants, or date quizzes tat test your ability to determine calendar weekdays.

To get started, enter a valid blueprint below and click Start. You will be presented with a quiz. After submitting your answers, you will see which ones were correct.
